## Title: **AI Boom**  

---

### Topic  
Exploring the Sustainability of Large-Scale AI Investments  

---

### Repository Overview  
This repository contains all materials for our CS7250 semester project, which investigates the sustainability of large-scale artificial intelligence (AI) investments. The project explores the long-term viability and potential outcomes of the build-out of massive AI infrastructures—focusing on economic and societal dimensions.

---

### Purpose  
The primary goal of this project is to analyze and critique current trends in AI investment, focusing on scalability, resource consumption, and societal impact. We aim to provide insights into whether these investments are sustainable and propose potential frameworks or recommendations for more responsible AI development.
